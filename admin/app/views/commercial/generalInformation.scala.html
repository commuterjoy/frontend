General Information

Special Ad Sizes

We have some special ad sizes that, if served, behave slightly differently

  • 1x1

    Ad slot will collapse

  • 300x251

    Ad slot will be 'sticky', i.e. will stay fixed to the top of the viewport as the user scrolls

  • 300x1050

    If this ad size is served, the most popular component in the right-hand column will be removed
